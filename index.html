Realtime Data that keeps you Safe

Proximity is that factor which tells us about the distance between tho objects. This data help the RC car to detect presecnce of objects and capture images in range of its proximity.

Temperature Sensors measure the amount of heat energy that is generated by an object or system, allowing us to “sense” the temperature producing either an analogue or digital output

A humidity sensor (or hygrometer) senses, measures and reports the relative humidity in the air and transmitts the data back for analysis. It therefore measures both moisture and air temperature.

Image recognition is the process of identifying and detecting an object or a feature in a digital image or video. This concept is used in many applications like theft detection and military services

Location tracking system is used for the observing and supplying a timely ordered sequence of location data for further processing. It sends latitude and longitude in the firebase database.

An emergency notification system is a method of facilitating the one-way dissemination or broadcast of messages to people, alerting them. This would be helpful in emergency case scenarios.
